Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • Garlic 2+ cloves
  • Salt and Pepper.
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Chicken Breast (1 per person should do it) diced
  • 1 Large Onion chopped
  • 1 Red Pepper chopped
  • 1 Tin Chopped Tomatoes
  • Cumin 1/2 teaspoon
  • Coriander 1 teaspoon
  • Turmeric 1 tablespoon
  • Chili Flakes 1 tablespoon
  • Gara Masala 1 teaspoon

Method

Tools

These are pretty essential for anything you cook.

  • At least one good large sharp knife
  • At least one wooden spoon
  • A Pestle and Mortar
  • A Colander
  • A good saucepan with a lid
  • A good size pan
  • A Seive

Top Tip

Get yourself one of these measuring spoons, they are super handy

Instructions

  • Peel and crush the garlic in the pestle and mortar
  • Peel and chop the onion
  • Chop the pepper
  • Measure out your spices and put them in a bowl
  • Put your saucepan over a medium heat and add a tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • Fry the garlic for a couple of minutes
  • Add the chopped onion to the garlic and fry for a couple of minutes
  • Add the chicken and the spices, fry for a around 5 minutes
  • Add the tin of tomatoes, bring to the boil and stir for around five minutes
  • Cover saucepan with lid, lower heat and leave it to simmer for 20 to 30 minutes
  • If you're having rice put that on the boil, once it's ready you are good to go, bon apetit!
